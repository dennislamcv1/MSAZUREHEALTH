# MSAZUREHEALTH
## Microsoft Azure AI in Healthcare Professional Certificate

### Professional Certificate - 4 course series

This certification helps you to learn how to design, deploy, and maintain AI-powered solutions that enhance clinical workflows, strengthen patient safety, and support better decision-making. You’ll build practical skills in healthcare-focused AI implementation, including natural language processing for clinical documentation, medical imaging analysis, predictive analytics for patient risk assessment, and secure data management using Azure services.Through hands-on guided projects and real clinical scenarios, you’ll practice applying Azure AI services such as Text Analytics for Health, Speech Services, Computer Vision, and Azure Machine Learning. You’ll build automation solutions that streamline documentation, surface key insights from patient data, and support care teams with intelligence. And you will do this while upholding healthcare compliance, ethics, and responsible AI principles.Whether you’re working in health IT, digital transformation, clinical informatics, or data analytics, this program gives you practical tools to contribute immediately to your role. This program bridges AI innovation with healthcare reality, helping you elevate both operational efficiency and patient experience while preparing for growing opportunities in clinical AI roles.Whether you're a healthcare professional, an IT specialist in a clinical setting, or a career changer looking to enter this dynamic field, this program gives you practical tools to contribute immediately to your role.
